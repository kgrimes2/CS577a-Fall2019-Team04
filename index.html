About

Team #4 has been tasked to work with FieldProgress in their efforts to advance democracy with smart mapping tooling.

Specifically, we will design and implement an algorithm that optimized turf cutting within precincts. Ultimately, our algorithm will be integrated into FieldProgress's platform.
